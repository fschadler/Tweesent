FAQs

What is Twitter Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment Analysis is a technique used in text mining. It may, therefore, be described as a text mining technique for analyzing the underlying sentiment of a text message, i.e., a tweet. Twitter sentiment or opinion expressed through it may be positive, negative or neutral.

How do you calculate the Sentiment Score?

For the calculation we use VaderSentiment. Before analyzing each Tweet, our programs strips all irrelevant data (Links, special characters, ...). After the evaluation of VaderSentiment we assign the Score back to the Tweets. As it can be seen our our Output Page we do additional calculations based of the output.

How does this website work?

You have to write a word or enter a hashtag for which you would like to get sentiments. You also have to enter the number of tweets and then click on submit button.
